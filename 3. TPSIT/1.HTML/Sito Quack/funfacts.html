Duck Fun Facts

Learn some amazing and quirky facts about ducks!

  • Ducks have webbed feet!

    Ducks have a special type of foot called a "palmate" foot, which has a web of skin between the toes. This helps them swim and dive more efficiently.

  • Ducks can fly up to 50 mph!

    Some duck species, such as the Mallard, can reach speeds of up to 50 mph when flying. That's almost as fast as a car on the highway!

  • Ducks have a special gland that helps them waterproof their feathers!</
